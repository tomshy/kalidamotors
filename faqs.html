FAQs: Clear Answers & Transparency

Find everything you need to know about importing your Japanese car to Kenya. We believe in complete transparency throughout the entire process.

After settling and purchasing the car, the entire importation process will take between 6 to 8 weeks. This includes 4-6 weeks for shipping from Japan to the Port of Mombasa, and 1-2 weeks for customs clearance and transportation to Nairobi or your preferred location. We provide regular updates throughout the process so you can track your vehicle every step of the way.
