The entire importation process usually takes between 4 to 6 weeks. This includes 2-3 weeks for shipping from Japan to the Port of Mombasa, and 1-2 weeks for customs clearance and transportation to Nairobi or your preferred location. We provide real-time tracking so you can monitor your vehicle's journey.
