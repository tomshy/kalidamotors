Maximize Your Budget

Smart importing can save you up to 30% on total landed costs. Follow these expert tips to get the most out of your money.

history_edu Target 7-Year Rule

Kenya allows imports up to 8 years old. Importing a vehicle that is exactly 7 years old often hits the "sweet spot" of low purchase price and manageable depreciation for tax purposes.

star_half Auction Grade 3.5/4

Focus on Grade 3.5–4 vehicles — mechanically sound, well-maintained cars with only minor signs of use. Japan’s grading is very strict, so even tiny, barely visible marks lower the score, letting you buy a reliable, high-quality car at a better price.

speed Engine CC

Keep an eye on engine capacity. Vehicles below 2000cc often fall into lower excise duty brackets, drastically reducing the final KRA bill.
