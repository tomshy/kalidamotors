help_outline Frequently Asked Questions

How much is the service fee?

expand_more

We maintain a fixed, transparent service fee structure. This fee covers dedicated sourcing, professional auction inspection, logistical management, and duty clearance support. There are zero hidden markups on the vehicle price.

What is the 5-vehicle inspection limit?

expand_more

To ensure we focus on the highest quality stock, we provide comprehensive on-the-ground physical inspections. This encourages focused selection and ensures our inspectors spend quality time on the best prospects.

What is the typical importation timeline?

expand_more

The process usually takes between 6 to 9 weeks. This includes auction winning (1 week), inland transit and export inspection in (2 weeks), shipping to Mombasa (4 weeks), and port clearance (1-2 weeks).

Can I inspect the car myself before paying duty?

expand_more

Yes, we facilitate viewings at the bonded warehouse in Mombasa. However, the purchase price and freight must be settled prior to shipping to Kenya as per international trade regulations.
